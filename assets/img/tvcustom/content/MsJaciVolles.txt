Background*
Ms. Jacqueline Gagnon-Volles, also known as Jaci, served as the Chief Marketing Officer of EveryWare Global, Inc. from April 2012 to February 19, 2014. Ms. Gagnon-Volles joined EveryWare in July 2011 as Vice President of Marketing. Prior to EveryWare, she worked at Home Products International from 2007 to 2011 as Executive Vice President Sales and Marketing. She served as the Chief Merchandising Officer for Lifetime Brands/ Pfaltzgraff responsible for the the Direct-to-Consumer division and Wholesales sales and marketing teams from January, 2004 to June 2007. Ms. Gagnon-Volles served as Vice President of Marketing at Libbey, Inc. and held several positions in the Anchor division of Newell Rubbermaid.
