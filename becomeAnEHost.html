Become An E-Host®

  1. Pages
  2. Host a Tuttle View Event
  3. Host a Tuttle View Event

Discover a NEW online shopping experience with a group of your friends or family.
Tuttle View Events are friendly, unique, stimulating and lots of fun.

Host a Tuttle View Event

CONNECT

Connect friends online for a one-hour E-Social® shopping event with Artisan products while you earn product dollars and special offers. Connect with friends around the country not just around the corner. A way to go shopping with your friends and earn product dollars for your wish list!

PERSONALIZE

Personalize your event choosing the product themes your friends will like. Select from Home, Kids, Jewelry & Accessories or Kitchen products. Your E-Curator® will present the products you pick together.

EASY AND FUN

The E-Host® invites friends to login and join the party at a specified time. The E-Curator® will run a personalized Tuttle View event for you and your friends. Host a party without cleaning your house or buying snacks. Have fun sharing ideas with friends while shopping online.

HOW IT WORKS

An E-Host® connects friends together for an E-Social® shopping event. Together, the E-Host® and E-Curator® will choose products, categories and artisans to be featured during the event based on the E-Host’s personal preferences.

  • The E-Host® shares the email addresses of her friends with the E-Curator® who then sends out an E-Vite to the group with instructions on to how to join the fun at a specified time for their personalized Tuttle View event.
  • The event includes live E-Curator® demonstrations, short videos, open chat room, engaging discussion and of course, shopping! All within one hour!
  • As an E-Host® you earn product dollars towards your Tuttle View wish list. You will have access to exclusive special offers and earn booking gifts if one of your friends becomes an E-Host®.

Tuttle View is a new way to have fun shopping online with friends from any location nation-wide and anytime!

Product dollars* based on sales at your event. Plus 50% off your favorite item.

E-HOST® REWARDS

Earn Product Dollars* based on sales at your event. Plus 50% off your favorite Tuttle View product.

  • $200 to $350 event = $25 to $30 (10% of sales)
  • $351 to $500 event = $53 to $75 (15% of sales)
  • $501 to $750 event = $100 to $150 (20% of sales)
  • $751 ++ event = 20% of sales plus 50% off 2 items

Special Purchases for E-Host® – You will access specially curated artisan products just for you!

Booking Gifts - Have your friends E-Host® an event and you earn product dollars when your friends host a qualified event (an event with over $200 in purchases). Earn:

  • 1 event = $10 product dollars
  • 2 events = $20 product dollars
  • 3 events = $30 product dollars

* Product dollars are used toward your Tuttle View store purchases.
