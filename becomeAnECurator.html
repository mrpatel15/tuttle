BECOME AN E-CURATOR™

Build a business FOR YOURSELF BUT NOT BY YOURSELF. Create your own online flexible E-Social™ shopping business that connects friends, enriches both your life and the life of others while celebrating entrepreneurs & skilled artisans.

YOUR SCHEDULE YOUR CHOICE

One hour E-Social™ shopping events FIT YOUR SCHEDULE. Earn money curating assortments that connect friends online, anywhere, nation-wide, at any time of day or night for FUN.

A Tuttle View online event means no extra house cleaning or travel.

A Tuttle View 1 hour E-vent™ versus a 2½ hour home party.

Tuttle View handles the transactions and delivers products directly to the E-host™ or customers home.

YOUR MONEY YOUR LIMITS

In this business the SKY'S THE LIMIT so you determine what works best for you. If it is a new career you are seeking, supplemental income you are looking for, retirement savings, or money for a family vacation, Tuttle View will help you achieve your goals. Make your dreams come true and have fun doing it.

•       Earn 20% sales commission. Tuttle View events online anywhere, any time, nation wide.

•       Unlimited coaching commissions for helping others become curators and for the curators they help. Build your team across the country!  Mentor and encourage others as they build their businesses as part of the Tuttle View model.

•       Personalized training with a mentor and 24/7 on-line resources.

•       All you need is a computer with a fast Internet connection and the desire to be part of this exciting and rewarding opportunity.

How much money you earn is up to you. What would you do with an extra $500 a month or MORE! What dreams would you make true?

 

YOUR BUSINESS YOUR WAY

•       Pick from a wide assortment of Artisans' products  : Home, Jewelry, Kids, Kitchen

•       Enrich the lives of others by bringing friends together around the country, not just around the corner.

•       Enrich the lives of others by coaching them to have their business.

•       No inventory to managed. Products delivered directly to the guests’ home or to your hostesses home.

•       Build your success kit with products from the stores you choose.
